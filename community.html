Our Community

More than a team - we're a family on the water

Welcome to the Dragon Family

Shadwell Dragons isn't just about paddling - it's about the people you paddle with. We're a diverse community of individuals who've found connection, purpose, and joy on the water together. From teenagers to retirees, office workers to artists, students to parents - we're united by our love of dragon boating and the friendships we've built.

Who Paddles With Us?

Our community reflects the vibrant diversity of East London:

👨‍💼
Working Professionals

Find balance and stress relief after work. Our evening sessions are perfect for decompressing and building connections outside the office.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
Families

Parents and kids paddling together, creating memories and healthy habits. Weekend sessions welcome all ages from 12+.

🎓
Students

Affordable fitness, new friends, and an exciting alternative to gym memberships. Special student rates available.

🏃
Fitness Enthusiasts

Push your limits with our competitive squad. Train hard, race harder, achieve together.

🌟
Social Paddlers

Here for the fun, friends, and fresh air. No pressure, just pleasure on the water.

👴
Active Seniors

Stay active and social. Our oldest paddler is 73 and still going strong!

Beyond Paddling

The boat is where we train, but the community extends far beyond:

  • Social Events: Monthly pub meet-ups, team dinners, and seasonal celebrations
  • Weekend Adventures: Trips to races across the UK and Europe
  • Volunteer Days: Giving back to Shadwell and local waterways conservation
  • Workshops: Nutrition, strength training, mental wellness sessions
  • Family Days: BBQs, picnics, and fun days for partners and kids

Community Values

🤗 Inclusivity

Everyone is welcome. We celebrate differences and create space for all.

🙌 Support

We lift each other up, on and off the water. Your success is our success.

🎉 Fun

We take our training seriously, but never ourselves. Laughter is essential.

💪 Growth

Whether it's technique, fitness, or confidence - we're all here to improve.

Member Stories

"I joined Shadwell Dragons six months after moving to London. I didn't know anyone, felt isolated, and needed something to pull me out of my shell. Dragon boating gave me a whole new friendship group and a reason to look forward to weekends. I've never felt more part of something." - Sarah, 28, joined 2 years ago
"My daughter started paddling at 14 and loved it so much that I decided to try too. Now it's our thing - we train together every Saturday. The team has watched her grow up, and the support she gets from everyone is incredible." - Michael, 46, paddling with daughter Emily
"After retiring, I needed something to keep me active and engaged. Dragon boating has given me both, plus friendships with people 40 years younger than me! Age is just a number in this boat." - Patricia, 68, joined 3 years ago
