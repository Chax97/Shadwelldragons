Our Community

Welcome to the Dragon Family

Shadwell Dragons isn't just about paddling - it's about the people you paddle with. We're a diverse community of individuals who've found connection, purpose, and joy on the water together. From teenagers to retirees, office workers to artists, students to parents - we're united by our love of dragon boating and the friendships we've built.

Beyond Paddling

The boat is where we train, but the community extends far beyond the water. We're friends, support systems, and adventure partners.

  • Social Events: Local BBQ's, team dinners, and seasonal celebrations
  • Volunteer Days: Giving back to Shadwell and local waterways conservation
  • Workshops: Nutrition, strength training, mental wellness sessions
  • Family Days: BBQs, picnics, and fun days for families and kids

Our Community Values

🤗
Inclusivity

Everyone is welcome. We celebrate differences and create space for all.

🙌
Support

We lift each other up, on and off the water. Your success is our success.

🎉
Fun

We take our training seriously, but never ourselves. Laughter is essential.

💪
Growth

Whether it's technique, fitness, or confidence - we're all here to improve.
