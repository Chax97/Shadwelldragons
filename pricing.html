Pricing FAQ

When do I start paying?

After your free taster session! Your first month starts when you officially join the team.

Can I change my membership level?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect the following month.

What about competition fees?

Race entry fees are separate and typically £15-40 per race depending on the event. Members get priority entry.

Is there a joining fee?

No joining fees! Just pay your first month's membership and you're part of the team.

How do I pay?

We accept direct debit, bank transfer, or card payment. Monthly payments are taken on the 1st of each month.

What's your cancellation policy?

One month's notice required. We're flexible - life happens, and we understand!
