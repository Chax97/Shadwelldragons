Welcome to Shadwell Dragons

We're a vibrant dragon boat racing team based in the heart of London's historic Shadwell Basin. Whether you're looking for a new fitness challenge, want to meet amazing people, or are searching for your next competitive sport - we've got a place for you.

Dragon boating is a spectacular team sport that combines power, rhythm, and camaraderie. In our boats, families paddle alongside fitness enthusiasts, beginners train with competitive athletes, and everyone pushes each other to be their best.
