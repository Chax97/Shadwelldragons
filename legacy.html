From Shadwell Solander to Shadwell Dragons

Scroll
01
1990s

The Solander Era

It started at Shadwell Basin with a group of friends and a shared heritage. We were Shadwell Solander, the first all-Bangladeshi crew to ever enter the British Dragon Boat Association competition.

Under Captain Vas Hirani and Coach Mike Thomas we transformed from local underdogs into a tactical powerhouse, climbing every division until we reached the Premier Division.

We weren't just paddling for trophies.
We were paddling for our identity.

02
1990s — 2022

The 25-Year Hibernation

The Shadwell Solander boys became men. Careers, families and responsibilities took over.

For nearly 25 years the paddles were stored away and the dragon lay dormant.

Our victories became stories told at weddings and family dinners — legends of our youth.

03
2022

The Birmingham Spark

Fate intervened at a festival in Birmingham.

A few original members stepped into a boat to help a local team — and suddenly the fire was back.

The muscle memory returned.
The rhythm of the drum took hold.
The hunger was reignited.

04
The Reunion

Shadwell Dragons

One afternoon in Birmingham sparked a movement.

Phone calls were made. Old teammates were tracked down.

The original crew assembled again — older, wiser and ready to rise.

We returned under a new name: Shadwell Dragons.

Not just to participate, but to pass our history to the next generation.
